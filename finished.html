Step 2: Power up the station

To power up your station you will need two 5V/3A power supplies: one with a USB-C connector for the Raspberry Pi and one with a male 5.5x2.1mm jack connector for the strobe illumination.

Use a voltage regulator to supply 3V to the strobe. Higher voltage can damage it.
