Individual holders

This is a modular and pluggable system designed for holding laboratory tubes.

They can easily be printed with PLA or other filament types (PTEG recommended for use in incubators). The dovetail slots ensure a perfect fit for all pieces. This models correspond to improvements to the original design from the DormantBiology Lab.

If you want to have access to the modifiable design on OnShape, then click here.

There are various options:

  • 0.2ml double.
  • 0.6ml double.
  • Cuvettes double thinner.
  • Cuvettes double thicker.
  • 1.5ml single.
  • 50ml single.
  • Cryo vial single.
