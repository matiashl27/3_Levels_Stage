Screen holder

Building

Parts

  • 1 7-inch capacitive touch screen for Raspberry Pi

Tools

  • 1 2.5mm Ball end Hex key

Mechanical Components

  • 4 M3 nuts
  • 4 M3x10mm cap head screws

Printed Parts

  • 1 Screen base
  • 1 Screen case
  • 1 Screen top

When it comes to the screen holder made for the 7-inch capacitive touch screen for Raspberry Pi, the process is quite straightforward.

All you need to do is follow a few simple steps. First, gather four M3x10mm cap head screws and four M3 nuts. Securely screw the corresponding parts onto the screen using the2.5mm Ball end Hex key , ensuring that each screw is tightly fastened and each nut is securely plugged into the Screen top and Screen base.

Once the parts are firmly in place, you can proceed to attach the Screen case. Simply align the protector with the screws and carefully affix it to ensure a snug fit. By following these steps, you'll be able to effectively install the screen holder and safeguard your device's display.
